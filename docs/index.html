How it works

Three steps. The third one is waiting.

01

Drop

Drag audio files — or a whole folder of them — onto the window. StenoDrop digs through subfolders and queues every audio file it finds.

02

Transcribe

Whisper runs locally on Apple silicon via Metal. A five-second clip takes under a second; nothing ever touches the network.

03

Read

Each transcript lands as name.txt beside its audio file — plus it's right there in the app to copy or reveal in Finder.

Why StenoDrop

A stenographer that lives on your Mac.

Cloud transcription services charge by the minute and read your audio on their servers. StenoDrop does the same job on your machine, for nothing.

Nothing leaves your Mac

Audio, transcripts, everything — processed and stored locally. Airplane mode works fine.

Folders, not files

Point it at a year of voice memos. It queues everything, works through the list, and skips what it's already seen in the batch.

Urdu → English, built in

First-class Urdu support with one-toggle translation to English — or keep the original script. 100 languages total.

Any audio you have

wav, mp3, m4a, flac, opus, voice memos — even the audio track of videos. ffmpeg normalizes everything first.

$0, no strings

Open source, no account, no trial, no per-minute meter. The Whisper model downloads once (~466 MB) and it's yours.

Metal-accelerated

whisper.cpp runs on the GPU. Batches that would cost hours of upload time finish while you make tea.

Speak in any language.
Read it in English.

Whisper's translate mode turns Urdu, Hindi, Arabic, Punjabi, Pashto — any of 100 languages — into clean English text. Or switch translation off and keep the transcript in its original script. Force a specific language when short clips fool auto-detection.

Install

Running in two minutes.

download — recommended
# 1. free engine + audio converter
brew install whisper-cpp ffmpeg

# 2. grab the app zip, unzip, move to Applications
github.com/fahdi/stenodrop/releases

# 3. unsigned build — clear quarantine once
xattr -cr /Applications/StenoDrop.app

First launch offers a one-click download of the Whisper model (~466 MB). After that you're fully offline.

build from source
git clone https://github.com/fahdi/stenodrop.git
cd stenodrop/mac
./scripts/make-app.sh
# → dist/StenoDrop.app, ad-hoc signed
cp -R dist/StenoDrop.app /Applications/

Plain Swift Package — no Xcode project. Requires macOS 14+ and Xcode command-line tools.
